  • What is the process to get a loan? 

    It’s fast, simple and online. To apply for a loan, simply start the application process by entering your details below. You will then be asked a series of questions about the property, the borrower, and other relevant details. Our online technology simplifies the process by only asking relevant questions about the specific property and situation. You only pay a one off loan origination fee when your loan is fully funded.

  • Why should I choose to use FundingPie over a traditional way of funding my projects?

    At FundingPie, we are able to complete funding quicker, with short term rates and only a one off loan origination fee. We offer complete transparency throughout the process. We take great care and pride in promoting the developer on our website, to our investors and giving the developer vast exposure to a large group of people who believe in the project, and want to see it succeed. You as the developer build your own brand. This is a new way of receiving funding, powered by technology in our connected and social world. Developers receive the benefits of all these forces combined, plus great customer service, and a team that is dedicated to ensuring the developer’s ultimate success.

  • How do borrowers benefit?

    Borrowers benefit from fast funding, competitive interest rates, automated systems, and a transparent process where the borrower knows where in the application process they are at all times through the borrower dashboard.

  • Can I live in the house I’m going to renovate?

    No. FundingPie loans are strictly non-owner occupied, Commercial Loans for the purpose of property development related activities. 

  • What types of properties do you lend on?

    Currently, we lend on residential 1-3 townhouse developments, small subdivisions, renovations and other residential up to the value of $2m. As we grow in the future, we will be looking at other larger projects.

  • What rates do you charge and how much cut do you take? 

    Rates are subject to a variety of factors including property location, loan size, experience of the borrower and other factors we look at. Get started with your application process today to get your custom rate. We charge the borrower up to 2% once the project is funded and that is all.

  • Where does the money to make the loans come from? 

    Loans are purchased by our online network of  private high net worth individuals. This is the new peer to peer economy.

  • How fast can I get funded?

    We can have your project approved within 7 days and then funded within max 30 days.
